Idea 1: Ghosty's Perlers

Ghosty's Perlers is an online store that sells custom fused perler bead items. Items include necklaces, bracelets, keychains, magnets, earrings, and different size posters.
This is mostly trageted to festivel goers and the rave community as perler necklaces and bracelets are worn to these events.
These items can also target kids, teens, and young adults as the other items can be bought as gifts.
As mentioned before, these items are customizable, meaning the buyers will submit a photo they want as a perler item.

Idea 2: Unique Cookies

Unique Cookies sell pre packaged cookie recipes. Each package would include the dry mix, flavoring, decorations, and any other baking needs.
The amount of ingredients and measurings in each recipe would depend on the "serving size" option the customer chooses.

Idea 3: Sip Bean

Sip Bean is a coffee inspired small business where they sell fun and customizable coffee cups.
Coffee cups include, regular hot and iced coffee cups, frappuccino cups (with the lid and straw), and espressor shot cups.
Each cup come in different sizes and are reusable as they can be used as Starbucks or any other coffee shop, even at home!
